About our Company Who we are, and what we do

Guaranteed Departures

Every departure is guaranteed! We can’t think of anything better to do than go skiing or camping, so our tours depart if there is one person booked or they are sold out. Relax knowing that your investment is safe and you can feel secure about booking travel around your tour.

Top Rated Company

We stop at nothing to make sure that your tour is the trip of a lifetime. From the moment you contact us and for the rest of your life you are an important part of our family. We care about the small things, as well as the big ones, so you can rest assured that your Fresh Adventures tour will be legendary. That’s why (proportionately) we get more 5 star reviews than any other tour company!

Top quality equipment

Your comfort is our priority so we use top quality equipment on our tours. We are partnered with some of the best gear companies for our camping gear, such as The North Face, Marmot, Pelican and Outdoor Research so you get the best on your adventure. Our fleet is regularly inspected and maintained so your tour runs smoothly!

We are serious about your safety!

We are here to keep you safe! We hire the most highly qualified guides who ensure your safety is a top priority every step of the way. Every tour is equipped with top notch communication, navigation and first aid equipment so we can handle any situation that comes our way.

Flexible Tour Options

We can make anything work for you! Don’t worry if your desired tour doesn’t match up perfectly with your flights etc. We can help you arrange your travel to and from the tour, adding hotels, shuttles, and even vacation packages to your booking. Let’s make this easy for you!
