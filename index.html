Important – Please read these terms before booking.

Everyone at K9PAWS and PawTours work very hard to provide you with one of the most memorable tours you will ever experience. Our dogs are also a huge part of each of our lives, so we understand the love you have for your best friend, or maybe friends! As much as we love our dogs, they are also all very well trained for the type of overwhelming and dangerous environments that any hiker or camper may, and probably will, encounter while out in the wilderness. It is important to know that we are NOT a training service. Every PawTours member is individually responsible for the handling, safety and wellness of their own dog(s). Accidents do happen, and we although we do have a well-trained medical staff on hand, it is important to come prepared just as you would for any backwoods camping trip.
